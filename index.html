Research direction

The symptoms are late. The signals come earlier.

By the time Alzheimer's disease is clinically diagnosed, significant neurodegeneration has already occurred. NeuroFlex is designed around a different premise: that subtle changes in how people interact with everyday digital tasks — reaction latency, hesitation, consistency, routine drift — may carry measurable signal years before the first symptom.

Digital biomarkers

Reaction time, task hesitation, completion patterns, memory recall curves, and behavioral consistency — captured passively through everyday app use.

Longitudinal trajectories

A single data point is noise. A trajectory spanning months is signal. NeuroFlex is built to study how behavioral patterns evolve — not just how they look at a single moment.

Privacy-first architecture

Anonymous identifiers, explicit consent, minimal personal data, and careful scientific language — built into the architecture from the start, not added as an afterthought.

From interaction to insight

Every tap is a data point. Every pattern is a hypothesis.

The product layer creates frequent, low-pressure interactions. The event layer beneath preserves the behavioral process — timing, hesitation, sequence — that turns individual actions into research-grade longitudinal data.

Six wellness pillars

Designed for support today. Built for measurement tomorrow.

Each activity serves a dual purpose: a meaningful cognitive or wellness interaction for the user, and a structured behavioral event for future research.

Train

Memory, attention, reaction time, and pattern recognition — brain games that create measurable cognitive performance signals over time.

Drink

Hydration reminders and completion tracking — simple habit loops that may later illuminate routine adherence and its disruption.

Move

Mobility, stretching, and walking prompts — designed for different physical ability levels, including older adults and caregivers.

Eat

Sustainable nutrition habits focused on behavior, not restriction — contribution to a longer-term picture of lifestyle regularity.

Connect

Social prompts and interaction check-ins — isolation is a known risk factor for cognitive decline; connection is the countermeasure.

Relax

Breathing, calming routines, and sleep-supportive prompts — stress response patterns are themselves a signal worth capturing.

Roadmap

A research platform built one phase at a time.

We are currently in Phase 1. The architecture is designed so that everything built now is a foundation — not a dead end.

Phase 1 · Now

First user-facing tool: a free offline Android wellness app with brain games, daily habit reminders, alarm system, and structured behavioral event architecture. No cloud, no account, no data collection.

Phase 2

Anonymous IDs, consent management, secure cloud sync, and behavioral event ingestion. Research-grade data pipeline with privacy-first design.

Phase 3

Longitudinal dataset development through sustained participation and partnerships with academic research institutions and neurologists.

Phase 4

AI and machine learning research — behavioral trajectory clustering, anomaly detection, sex-stratified models, and hypothesis generation for early biomarker identification.
