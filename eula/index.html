1. What NeuroFlex Is

NeuroFlex is a free, non-commercial cognitive wellness application currently in alpha development. It is designed to support healthy cognitive habits through brain training games, daily routine reminders, an alarm system, and simple wellness activities across six categories: Train, Drink, Move, Eat, Connect, and Relax.

NeuroFlex is also being designed with a long-term research direction in mind. The architecture is intended to support future longitudinal behavioral research into early digital biomarkers associated with cognitive decline — in particular Alzheimer's disease. This research direction is aspirational and subject to separate consent, ethics review, and institutional partnership agreements before any research data collection begins.

In the current version, NeuroFlex does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All information you enter or create in the app remains on your device. There are no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.
